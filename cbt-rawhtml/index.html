Chainbridge Technologies: A History of Success
Chainbridge Technologies History

 Chainbridge Technologies, a software company located in Tysons Corner, VA, was founded in 2003. Since inception, Chainbridge has worked to meet the market need for timely and effective database applications. Focusing on scientific and emergency management solutions, Chainbridge has over a 14-year track record of delivering mission-critical, operations-centric systems.

Key differentiators with our systems include expert data architechure, intuitive user interface and work flow, the data access privilege platform SQLDBI, blast communications (phone, text, email) and cloud hosting if required.

Our current lines of business are emergency management and mission preparedness systems, grant management systems, Microsoft Azure support services, Deltek professional services, and line-of-business application implementations.
